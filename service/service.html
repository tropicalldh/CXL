Information Session:

  • An Information Session can be arranged at the request of an Applicant or a Guardian before Enrolment.
  • A Teacher can visit the Applicant or the Guardian with no fee incurred.
  • The final decision on arrangement of an Information Session lies with RectiCurve.

Class Capacity Limit:

  • Class Capacity Limit: In compliance with section 3(1) of the Education Ordinance in Hong Kong SAR, SeedCoding's class capacity is strictly controlled to be less than 8 students per class and less than 20 students per day because RectiCurve is a small group tutoring service provider, not "school". Enrolment:

  • Enrolment consists of two types: 1) Commencement of Enrolment, and 2) Maintenance of Enrolment.
  • Commencement of Enrolment takes effect when the Applicant 1) has got an Offered Place and 2) completes a full payment of the Tuition before or on the Due Date.
  • After Commencement of Enrolment completed, the Applicant becomes Student.
  • Maintenance of Enrolment takes effect when the Student 1) has got an Offered Place and 2) completes a full payment of the Tuition before or on the Due Date.
  • In relation to Commencement of Enrolment, Places are offered to the Applicants on a first-apply-first-offered basis. If an Applicant fails to put the Commencement of Enrolment in effect, the Offered Place can change into a Place available to any other Applicant.
  • In relation to Maintenance of Enrolment, Places are offered to the existing Students of the Course first. If a Student fails to put the Maintenance of Enrolment in effect, the Student loses its Student status and the Offered Place can change into a Place available to any other Applicant.
  • If there is an available Place, the Place can be offered to any Applicant at RectiCurve’s discretion at any time unless otherwise specified.
    • Exception: In relation to Private Courses, the consent of all the existing Students (or initiating Applicants if the Enrolment is not yet commenced) is required in order to offer any available Place to any other Applicant than them.
  • Enrolled Period is set to one month (which means the Maintenance of an Enrolment is carried out on a monthly basis) unless otherwise specified by RectiCurve.

Tuition:

  • For each Enrolled Period, the Tuition can be revised reflecting changes in the number of the Students and the like.
  • The amount and calculation logic of the offered Tuition is determined by RectiCurve at its full discretion.
