About SeedCoding

SeedCoding is a coding education brand of RectiCurve. It provides coding classes for the young in Hong Kong.

Meaning of SeedCoding

  • We Seed Coding Skills:

    • We seed coding skills by providing coding classes.

  • STEMs Come from a Seed:

    • Coding facilitates learning STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) by helping students directly implement, test and simulate what they learn and what they come up with.

  • The Class is for Seeds:

    • The class is for the young who will grow up in a variety of ways along with their own talents in the future. We believe learning how to code in the early age will help them fully display their talents in many different fields where they may play through their lives.

About RectiCurve

RectiCurve manages SeedCoding.

Story of RectiCurve

RectiCurve is a startup incorporated in Hong Kong on 15 August 2017 to launch a new online advertising platform first in Hong Kong and next to the all over the world.

To put legal protection on its business idea, RectiCurve had prepared for a patent application during the 2nd half of 2017, and successfully applied for a PCT patent on 1 January 2018.

The founder & CEO, Tony Lee, is now developing all the relevant systems by himself and now is going to share with the young what he has learned about coding since 2005 and what he thinks should be learned together with coding, such as knowledge of mathematics.
